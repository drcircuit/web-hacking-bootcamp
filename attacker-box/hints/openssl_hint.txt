AES-128-ECB (no IV):
    openssl enc -d -aes-128-ecb -K <hexkey> -in ciphertext.bin -out output.txt -nosalt -nopad

AES-256-CBC (with IV):
    openssl enc -d -aes-256-cbc -K <hexkey> -iv <hexiv> -in ciphertext.bin -out output.txt -nosalt
